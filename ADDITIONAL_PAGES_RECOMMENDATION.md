## 📄 Recommended Additional Pages

For a complete e-commerce website, here are essential pages you should consider:

### **Priority 1: Legal & Trust Pages** (Essential for E-commerce)

#### 1. **Shipping & Delivery Policy** ⭐
**Why you need it:**
- Required by law for e-commerce
- Builds customer trust
- Reduces inquiry volume
- Clear expectations management

**What to include:**
- Delivery timeframes (e.g., "5-7 business days within India")
- Shipping charges (Free above ₹500? Flat ₹50?)
- Areas covered (Pan-India? International?)
- Courier partners (BlueDart, Delhivery, etc.)
- Order tracking information
- Packaging details

**Sample content:**
```
Shipping Policy

Delivery Time:
- Metro cities: 3-5 business days
- Other cities: 5-7 business days
- Remote areas: 7-10 business days

Shipping Charges:
- Free shipping on orders above ₹499
- Flat ₹50 for orders below ₹499

We ship pan-India through reliable courier partners.
```

---

#### 2. **Return & Refund Policy** ⭐
**Why you need it:**
- Legal requirement for e-commerce
- Reduces disputes
- Builds customer confidence
- Clear problem resolution process

**What to include:**
- Return window (7 days? 15 days?)
- Conditions for returns (unopened? damaged?)
- Refund process and timeline
- Who pays return shipping?
- Exchange options
- Non-returnable items

**Sample content:**
```
Return & Refund Policy

Return Window: 7 days from delivery

Eligible for Return:
- Damaged/defective products
- Wrong item delivered
- Missing items

Non-returnable:
- Opened/used products (hygiene reasons)
- Products without original packaging

Refund: Within 7-10 business days to original payment method
```

---

#### 3. **Privacy Policy** ⭐
**Why you need it:**
- Legal requirement
- GDPR compliance (if selling internationally)
- Protects your business
- Builds trust

**What to include:**
- What data you collect (name, email, phone, address)
- How you use the data
- Who you share it with (payment processors, couriers)
- How you protect data
- User rights (access, deletion)
- Cookie policy

**Tip:** You can use free generators:
- https://www.privacypolicygenerator.info/
- https://www.freeprivacypolicy.com/

---

#### 4. **Terms & Conditions** ⭐
**Why you need it:**
- Legal protection for your business
- Defines user obligations
- Limits liability
- Sets dispute resolution process

**What to include:**
- Website usage terms
- Purchase terms
- Payment terms
- Intellectual property rights
- Limitation of liability
- Governing law (Indian law)
