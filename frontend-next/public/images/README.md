# Image Folders

## Directory Structure

- **logos/** - Contains brand logos (Addrika logo, company logo)
- **products/** - Contains product fragrance photos

## Upload Your Images Here

### Logos Folder
Place your logo files in `logos/`:
- addrika-logo.png (your brand logo)

### Products Folder  
Place your product photos in `products/`:
- kesar-chandan.jpg
- regal-rose.jpg
- oriental-oudh.jpg
- bold-bakhoor.jpg

## After Uploading

1. Ensure filenames match exactly (case-sensitive)
2. Restart the frontend: `sudo supervisorctl restart frontend`
3. Refresh your website to see changes
